About

Hey There,

My name is Becca and I am a UI Designer with a background in Graphic Design. My main focus is on creating unique and personalized user experiences for digital projects.

When I'm not designing, you can find me supporting my favorite team at a sporting event in Cincinnati, Ohio - Go Bengals! Or taking advantage of the numerous parks, restaurants and breweries that this amazing city has to offer.

Although the people of Cincinnati may love their Skyline or Goldstar, I must admit that it's not for me and you won't catch me chugging it at a Bengals game.
